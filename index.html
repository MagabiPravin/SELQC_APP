Help

1. New Report: Fill out the form to create a new site report. Report Name, Project Name, Engineer's Name, and Site Location are required.

2. View Reports: Access all your saved reports. Click on a report to open its editor.

3. Report Editor: Add photos, view report details, and generate a PDF report. Click on a photo to edit it.

4. Photo Editor: Annotate your photos with drawings, text, shapes, and arrows. You can also add a description. Click the save button to keep your changes.

5. Delete Photo: To delete a photo, click the red 'X' button that appears on the photo thumbnail in the Report Editor screen.

6. PDF Generation: Click "Generate PDF" to create a professional report document. The PDF will include a stylish cover page with engineer details and all your annotated photos with their descriptions.
